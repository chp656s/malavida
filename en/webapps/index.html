Webapps

The evolution of web browsers has led to a trend within the world of software: the so-called webapps, developments capable of running on programs such as Opera, Firefox, Google Chrome or Internet Explorer.

Despite including fewer options than their desktop counterparts, web apps (that can be launched from iOS, Windows, Linux, OS X and Android) have the advantage of being immune to the majority of malware strains, not occupying any hard drive space and not using up too many resources to work correctly.

Video games, mail clients, social networks, image editors and even invoicing and financial management programs can run as web apps and are a great alternative to certain desktop programs.
