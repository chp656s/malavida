iOS

iOS is Apple’s mobile operating system created by Steve Jobs and, although it was originally developed for the iPhone (iPhone Operating System), it’s currently used on many more devices such as the iPad, iPod Touch, or the new Apple Watches.

iOS is a closed operating system intended for digital used and based on multi-touch gestures. Its arrival on the market’s first iPhone meant a true revolution to how users interacted with their mobile devices, changing this industry forever.
