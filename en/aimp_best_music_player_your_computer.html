Aimp Player en

Aimp - this is the best free audio player for listening to music on your computer. The player aimp has built-in codecs, and immediately after the installation of the program you can listen to music in *. MP3, *. MP2, *. MP1, *. MPC, *. MP +, *. AAC, *. AC3, *. OGG, *. FLAC , *. APE, WavPack, Speex, *. WAV, *. CDA, *. WMA, *. S3M, *. XM, *. MOD, *. IT, *. MO3, *. MTM, *. UMX file formats.

Aimp player allows you to play streaming radio, listen to radio online, simply drag the file with the address of the stream in the playlist player. If you have audio files are opened by another player, then go to player settings (in the left corner click on the wrench or key combination "Ctrl + P"), go to the tab "File Association" and tick against the files with the extensions. Also in the settings you can change the look of the player (color cover), change your hotkeys, sound effects to remove voice from song.
